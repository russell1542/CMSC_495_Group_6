Welcome to 1st Class Body!

First Class Body is all about helping beginners work their way into living a healthier lifestyle. Our goal is to make working out easier for everyone by providing multiple workout plans and a log to track progress from day one. Our workout plans were also built for working out both in a gym and at home with dumbbells. These plans allow individuals who work full time to save time driving to and from a gym as well as providing privacy. As a beginner it is easy to be intimidated in a gym full of people who seem to know what they are doing. The privacy that our workout plans offer save beginners from being intimidated, giving them a smaller chance of quiting due to insecurities. Take a look at the three plans that we offer to gain muscle weight, lose weight, or get toned.
