Welcome to our Lose Weight plan!

If you are a begginer looking to lose weight this is the plan for you. We pieced together a full body workout plan that alternates what you do each day of the week. This gives your body a balance of strength and cardio work that allows you to see the weight loss. Below are two workout routines A and B. What we want you to do is alternate the workouts every other week, Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. So for your first week follow Workout A on Monday, Workout B on Wednesday, and Workout A again on Friday. Then for week two switch it up and do Workout B on Monday, then Workout A on Wednesday, and Workout B again on Friday. This gives your muscles at least one rest day in between workouts, as well as working out each muscle group evenly.
