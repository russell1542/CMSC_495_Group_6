Welcome to our Get Toned plan!

If you are looking to tone up your muscles but don't know where to start, this plan is for you. First Class Body has pieced together a full body workout plan that concentrates on muscle gain. The best way to see results quicker is to add in some form of cardio such as running, biking, jumping jacks, etc. We recommend following this workout plan at least three days a week with a rest day in between.



Warm Up

The best way to warm up your muscles is by doing some form of cardio workout. Some great examples are running, biking, and using the elliptical. If you do not have access to do any of these jumping jacks are a great alternative. Anythink that gets your body moving and your heart rate up will improve the results of that days workout routine.
