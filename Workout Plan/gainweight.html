Welcome to our Gain Weight plan!

When most people hear the words "gain weight" they cringe and think who wants to gain weight? With First Class Body, we help you gain weight in a healthy way by building out your muscles with a full body workout plan. If you do choose this plan, keep in mind that when working on gaining muscle you have to eat more protien to help heal your muscles while making sure you eat enough carbs to keep up your energy. You might also ask, How do I know what amought of weight works for me? The answer to this is start out with no weight to get the form of the workout down first. Once you have the correct form down then start adding weight. If the weight you are using is still too light keep adding weight. If it gets to the point that your form is no longer correct, you need to go back to the previous weight and that is your starting weight. You will base all future workouts on this starting weight. We highly recommend that you use our workout log (or a notebook) to keep track of the weight you use for each workout. Now you know what to do to get started today!
